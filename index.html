The Passion Planner was created by Angelia Trinidad after she graduated from college back in 2012. She designed the passion planner to combat anxiety. With the intent to create a personal organizer to help simplify your life and focus on what really matters.

Features
  1. Passion Roadmap

    2. It is a step-by-step guide for mapping out your goals. Set a timer for 5 minutes and imagine anything you write on the page will come true.

  2. GameChanger

    3. What is the goal that would have the most positive impact on your life right? This is your Gamechanger. It is the detailed steps that you'll need to complete to your goal.

  3. Monthly Layouts

    4. Plan each month's goals and events so you always know what's coming have the Big picture overview of your progress towards your goals.

  4. Weekly Layouts

    5. Manage your weekly schedule and work toward your goals on a daily basis using weekly layouts. Dedicate time at the beginning of each week to prepare.
